Everything you need to know

What format are the files and do I need special software?
All tools are delivered as Google Sheets and Notion templates — they work entirely in your browser. No software to install, no subscription needed beyond a free Google and Notion account. You receive a personal copy of every file that belongs only to you.
Do I need a financial background to use these tools?
No. Every tool is designed for café owners, not accountants. Blue cells are where you enter numbers — everything else calculates automatically. An instructions guide walks you through each tool. If you can operate a till, you can use these files.
Can I upgrade from the Operations Pack to the Full Guide later?
Yes. If you purchase the Operations Pack first and later decide to upgrade to the Full Guide, you pay only the difference — not the full price again. The amount you already paid is credited toward the upgrade.
How do I receive my files after purchasing?
Immediately after purchase you receive access to your personal Welcome Hub — a page containing links to every tool. Click each link to make a personal copy to your Google Drive or Notion. The entire process takes under five minutes.
Is this relevant if my café is already running?
Especially relevant. Most café owners who already have a running business discover that the Operations Pack shows them exactly where they have been losing money without knowing it. The tools work from day one for new owners and from day one for experienced ones.
