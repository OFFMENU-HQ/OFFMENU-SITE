Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — plainly explained.
Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

OFF MENU provides operational guides and tools for café and food-business owners. This website and its services are operated by OFF MENU , based in Greece. For anything related to your data, contact us at offmenu.business@gmail.com.

What data we collect, and why

Your email address — when you request our free guide or subscribe to updates, we collect the email address you provide. We use it to deliver the guide, send you relevant OFF MENU content, and occasionally tell you about our products. Legal basis: your explicit consent, given via the checkbox on our signup form. You can withdraw it at any time.

Purchase information — if you buy one of our products, our payment provider processes your payment details. We never see or store your card numbers. We receive your email address and order details so we can deliver what you bought.

Basic technical data — our hosting provider (Cloudflare) automatically processes standard technical information such as IP addresses to serve the website securely and protect it from abuse. We do not use this to identify you.

Who processes your data on our behalf

  • Kit (ConvertKit) — our email service provider. Stores subscriber emails and sends our emails. Kit is based in the United States and participates in recognised EU–US data-transfer frameworks.
  • Cloudflare — hosts and secures this website.
  • Payhip — processes purchases and payments when you buy a product.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with anyone beyond the processors above, who act under our instructions.

How long we keep your data

We keep your email address until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it. Purchase records are kept as long as required by Greek tax and accounting law. Technical logs are retained briefly by our hosting provider for security purposes.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct it if it's wrong
  • Have it deleted ("right to be forgotten")
  • Receive a copy in a portable format
  • Withdraw your consent at any time — every email we send includes an unsubscribe link that works instantly
  • Lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (dpa.gr) if you believe we've mishandled your data

To exercise any of these, email offmenu.business@gmail.com — we'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This website currently uses only the strictly necessary technical cookies set by our hosting and security provider. We do not run advertising or tracking cookies on this site. If that changes — for example, if we add analytics or advertising pixels — this policy and our cookie practices will be updated first, and consent will be requested where required.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle your data, we'll update this page and revise the date at the top. Significant changes affecting existing subscribers will be communicated by email.
