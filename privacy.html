Who we are

OFF MENU creates operational guides and tools for café and food-business owners. This website and its services are operated by OFF MENU, based in Greece.

For anything related to your personal data, we are the "data controller" — which simply means we're the ones responsible for handling it properly, and the ones you contact about it.

What we collect, and why

WhatWhen & why
Your email address When you request the free guide or subscribe to updates. We use it to deliver the guide, send you useful OFF MENU content, and occasionally tell you about our products. Legal basis: your explicit consent — the checkbox you tick on the form. You can withdraw it at any time.
Purchase details When you buy a product, our payment provider processes the payment. We never see or store your card numbers. We receive your email and order details so we can deliver what you bought and keep the records Greek tax law requires.
Basic technical data Our hosting provider automatically processes standard technical information — such as IP addresses — to serve the website securely and protect it from attacks. We don't use this to identify you.

That's the complete list. We don't collect names, phone numbers, locations, or anything else — because we don't need them.

Who processes it for us

A few carefully chosen services handle data on our behalf, under our instructions:

  • Kit (ConvertKit) — our email service. Stores subscriber emails and sends our emails. Kit is based in the United States and participates in recognised EU–US data-transfer frameworks.
  • Cloudflare — hosts and secures this website.
  • Payhip — processes purchases and payments when you buy a product.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers, brokers, or anyone beyond the processors above.

How long we keep it

  • Your email: until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it.
  • Purchase records: as long as Greek tax and accounting law requires us to keep them.
  • Technical logs: kept briefly by our hosting provider for security, then discarded.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you can:

  • Access — ask for a copy of everything we hold about you.
  • Correct — have anything wrong put right.
  • Delete — have everything erased ("the right to be forgotten").
  • Take it with you — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent — any time. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link that works instantly.
  • Complain — to the Hellenic Data Protection Authority at dpa.gr if you believe we've mishandled your data.

To use any of these, email us — details at the bottom of this page. We respond within 30 days, and exercising your rights costs nothing.

Cookies

This website uses only the strictly necessary technical cookies set by our hosting and security provider to keep the site working and safe. We run no advertising or tracking cookies — which is why you don't see a cookie banner here.

If that ever changes — for example, if we add analytics or advertising tools — this policy will be updated first, and we'll ask for your consent where the law requires it.

How we protect it

The website is served entirely over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Your email lives inside Kit's secured infrastructure, and payment data never touches our systems at all — it goes directly to the payment provider, which is certified to handle it.

No system on earth is perfectly secure, but we deliberately keep the amount of data we hold as small as possible — the safest data is the data we never collected.

Children

Our products and website are intended for business owners and adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has given us their details, contact us and we'll delete them promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle your data, we'll update this page and revise the date at the top. If a change meaningfully affects existing subscribers, we'll also tell you by email before it takes effect.
